The point of this programme was to explore the definition of a polymath.
The Shorter Oxford English Dictionary says it means “a person of much or varied learning”.
You have to be one of those if you’re going to compete on a quiz show where the questions can be about anything, from anthropology to zoology.
Monkman and Seagull fit that bill.
University Challenge chairman Jeremy Paxman said “Correct” to their answers so often his sneer froze.
